Point of View

7 July 2021

Like every other teenager, I procrastinate. A LOT. I always waited till the very last minute to do the work I had to do, I was busy - with other things.

But once in a while this moment of reality hit happens, where I realize I'm behind with everything, clean up some room for the things I have to do and go on with the same cycle. Slowly the plan fades away, the casual procrastination comes in and the things are finished (sometimes aren't) in the very last minute.

This was the case till I had in my hands a really big project, the biggest I had ever had to make and guess what happened - it failed. Since than I officially declared war on procrastination and the things that cause it. This was 2 years ago, since than I have learned a lot and wanna share a lot about it

Various versions have evolved over the years, sometimes by accident.

Samuel Bushi
